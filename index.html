Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf 2026.

Who can attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf is open to software engineers, students, designers, founders, researchers, and anyone passionate about technology. Whether you're just starting your journey or have years of experience, you'll find sessions tailored to your interests.

What's included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes access to keynote sessions, technical talks, workshops (depending on your plan), networking events, refreshments, conference swag, and digital session recordings.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All attendees receive access to conference recordings. Recording availability depends on the ticket tier you choose.

Can I transfer my ticket?

Yes. Tickets may be transferred to another attendee before the registration deadline by contacting the event support team.

Where will the conference take place?

DevConf 2026 will be hosted at a modern convention center with multiple presentation halls, workshop rooms, networking lounges, food courts, and dedicated sponsor areas.

How can I contact the organizers?

Our support team is available through email and the official event website. We'll respond to all attendee questions as quickly as possible before and during the conference.
